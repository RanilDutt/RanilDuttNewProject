What is your interest in IT

I'm interested in IT because I'm extremely interested in learning and excelling at new technologies. I've mastered some programs and languages in my current job as a Tester and want to expend my knowledge in IT and I look forward to mastering more programs as they're developed. I'm also interested in creative problem solving and developing large Application systems. My interest in IT started when I started working in a large organisation in system team. I have many interesting developers that I work with on a day to day basis. Looking at their skills in development has inspired me to study further in IT.

Why did you choose to come to RMIT

As an Open University Student I was looking to do a course in IT online and RMIT has that option and also I came from a Business background and I can choose a minor in Business with this course. Upon doing some research on universities RMIT showed a track record in preparing students for success, exposing them to new ideas and equipping them with skills and knowledge to succeed in their chosen career. I found that of all the universities RMIT had the best reputation the field I wanted to work in(IT).
